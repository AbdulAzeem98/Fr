About Python

Python is a high-level, interpreted programming language known for its readability and simplicity. It supports multiple programming paradigms, including procedural, object-oriented, and functional programming. Python is widely used in various domains such as web development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, scientific computing, and more.

Python's extensive standard library and active community contribute to its popularity, making it a versatile choice for both beginners and experienced developers. It is often praised for its ease of learning and the ability to quickly develop applications.
